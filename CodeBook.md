The tidy data set only retains measurements on the mean and standard deviation for each measurement from the source data. The data are then averaged per each subject and activity. 

There are 6 types of activities. 
1. WALKING
2. WALKING_UPSTAIRS
3. WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS
4. SITTING
5. STANDING
6. LAYING
